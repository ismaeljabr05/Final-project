about us

Astronauts' website offers a unique experience for simulating a space visit using virtual reality (VR) technology. This platform allows users to explore the universe in an innovative and immersive way without leaving Earth. Through advanced VR headsets, visitors can enjoy viewing planets, stars, and galaxies, and even experience walking on the surface of the Moon or Mars. The experience is designed to be both educational and entertaining, providing detailed scientific information about space, making it suitable for all ages. about team

The "Astronauts" website was founded by Sadeen , Ismail , and Somaya , who are passionate about astronomy and space. The idea for the site originated from the desire to offer a unique experience that allows users to explore the universe and learn more about space using the latest technologies such as virtual reality. The main goal of creating the site was to make astronomical knowledge more accessible and provide an interactive experience that combines education and entertainment, helping to stimulate scientific curiosity and deepen understanding of the wonders of the universe.
