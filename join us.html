Space Science Festival

This festival was held at Mecca Mall in Amman from October 28 to 31, 2024. It featured exhibitions of spacecraft and rockets,interactive shows, and workshops aimed at enhancing knowledge of space science and astronomy among youth and families.

Youth Space and Astronomy Club

This club event was held on May 30, 2024, offering young participants the chance to explore the wonders of the universe and learn the basics of astronomy through interactive workshops and lectures delivered by experts in the field.

Space and Astronomy Club Project

The Space and Astronomy Club project aims to inspire young people with a passion for space and astronomy through educational and interactive activities. The club offers workshops, lectures, and stargazing sessions using advanced telescopes, along with hands-on activities such as designing rocket models and exploring planets and stars. Targeting the 12–18 age group, the project seeks to deepen their understanding of the universe's wonders, develop their scientific and innovative skills, and contribute to building a generation passionate about science and discovery.
